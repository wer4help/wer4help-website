Impact & Achievements

Key milestones and achievements of WeR4Help.

Club Banner Relaunch

The club banner was redesigned and relaunched to represent the renewed vision and identity of WeR4Help, strengthening its presence within the campus community.

Launch of Aashraya Connect

Aashraya Connect was introduced as a platform to expand the outreach of the club and strengthen connections with social initiatives and communities in need.

Record Breaking Tenure

This tenure marked one of the most active periods of the club with a record number of initiatives, volunteer participation, and impactful activities conducted throughout the year.

Institutional Domain Email

The club officially adopted an institutional domain email (wer4help@gvpce.ac.in), strengthening its professional identity and communication with organizations.

Media Recognition
  • Newspaper Coverage
  • Earned a feature on YouTube
Donation of a Table Fan

A table fan was donated to support the needs of a community member, reflecting the club’s commitment to meaningful and practical assistance.
