Acknowledgment

A hearty welcome to the WeR4Help Club Website. Looking back, our club laid its foundation in 2011. Since then, every tenure has marked a new chapter of learning, leadership, and commitment to social service. Each team has contributed to strengthening the club while passing the torch forward with the same dedication, energy, and sense of responsibility. As we all know, WeR4Help is a self-owned student club. It is a matter of great pride that our club has been formed by students, run by students, and led by students of GVPCE from 2011 to the present day. During my tenure, I strongly believed that leadership should go beyond merely repeating the initiatives of previous years. My focus was on introducing meaningful initiatives that could create a long-term impact for future generations of the club. From this vision emerged the idea that every tenure, event, and team contribution should be digitally documented and accessible so that anyone connected to WeR4Help can explore our journey and accomplishments. This vision led to the development of the WeR4Help Club Website a platform where the history, initiatives, and contributions of each tenure can be preserved and accessed by everyone. During this tenure we also worked towards obtaining an institutional domain email ID for the club and launched Aashraya Connect, a structured survey initiative aimed at creating organized records and long-term data for our social service activities. Together with T. Sai Shashank and P. Keerthana, we collected the necessary information and developed this website with the vision that the initiatives of our club should be digitally accessible to everyone. We extend our sincere gratitude to Dr. A. B. Koteswara Rao, Principal and Director of CFSR, and Dr. C. V. Nageswara Rao, Associate Director of CFSR, for their encouragement and support. We also thank our Faculty Coordinator Ms. Lateefa Shaik for her guidance and support. A special appreciation goes to T. Sai Shashank - Club Advisor and P. Keerthana - Promotions Lead (2025–2026), for helping bring this website from concept to completion. With great pride, we officially launch the WeR4Help Club Website on 15 March 2026, marking another milestone in the journey of our club.

Yours sincerely,

M. Anush

Student President
WeR4Help 2025–26
