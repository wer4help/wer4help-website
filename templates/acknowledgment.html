Acknowledgment

A hearty welcome to the WeR4Help Club Website.

Looking back, our club laid its foundation in 2011. Since then, every tenure has marked a new chapter of learning, leadership, and commitment to social service.

As we all know, WeR4Help is a self-owned student club.

During my tenure, I strongly believed that leadership should go beyond merely repeating the initiatives of previous years.

This vision led to the development of the WeR4Help Club Website.

Together with T. Sai Shashank and P. Keerthana, we collected the necessary information and developed this website.
